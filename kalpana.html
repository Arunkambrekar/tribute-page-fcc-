Kalpana Chawla

First Indian-born woman in space

Journey of a Star:

  • 1962 - Born in Karnal, Haryana, India
  • 1982 - Earned Bachelor of Engineering degree in Aeronautical Engineering
  • 1988 - Earned PhD in Aerospace Engineering from University of Colorado
  • 1995 - Selected by NASA for astronaut training
  • 1997 - First space mission aboard Space Shuttle Columbia (STS-87)
  • 2000 - Selected for her second space mission
  • 2003 - Final mission aboard STS-107, which ended in tragedy
"The path from dreams to success does exist. May you have the vision to find it, the courage to get on to it, and the perseverance to follow it." — Kalpana Chawla
