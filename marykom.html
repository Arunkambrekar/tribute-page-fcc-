M.C. Mary Kom

Magnificent Mary - Queen of Indian Boxing

Championship Journey:

  • 1983 - Born in Kangathei, Manipur
  • 2000 - Started boxing training
  • 2001 - First international medal (Silver)
  • 2002 - First World Championship gold
  • 2012 - Olympic bronze medal (London)
  • 2014 - Asian Games gold medal
  • 2018 - Commonwealth Games gold
  • 2020 - Padma Vibhushan award
