Dr. Verghese Kurien

Father of India's White Revolution

Timeline of India's Milk Revolution:

  • 1921 - Born in Kozhikode, Kerala
  • 1949 - Joined Anand dairy project
  • 1955 - Established Kaira District Cooperative
  • 1965 - Founded Amul brand
  • 1970 - Launched Operation Flood
  • 1973 - Established National Dairy Development Board
  • 1999 - Received World Food Prize
  • 2012 - Passed away at age 90
"India's place in the sun would come from the partnership between wisdom of its rural people and skill of its professionals." — Dr. Verghese Kurien
