Satyajit Ray

Maestro of Indian Cinema

Cinematic Legacy:

  • 1921 - Born in Calcutta
  • 1955 - Debut film "Pather Panchali"
  • 1959 - "Apur Sansar" completes Apu Trilogy
  • 1961 - "Teen Kanya" anthology released
  • 1977 - Created detective Feluda
  • 1987 - Received France's Legion of Honor
  • 1992 - Honorary Oscar for Lifetime Achievement
  • 1992 - Passed away in Calcutta
"The director is the only person who knows what the film is about." — Satyajit Ray
