Arunima Sinha

First female amputee to summit Mount Everest

Against All Odds:

  • 1989 - Born in Ambedkar Nagar, UP
  • 2011 - Lost leg in train accident
  • 2012 - Began mountaineering training
  • 2013 - Summited Everest (first female amputee)
  • 2014 - Awarded Padma Shri
  • 2015 - Summited Kilimanjaro
  • 2019 - Completed Seven Summits challenge
  • 2021 - Published autobiography "Born Again on the Mountain"
"I decided I wouldn't cry over what I'd lost, but fight for what I still had." — Arunima Sinha
